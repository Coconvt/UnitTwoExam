Carrots!

Here are a few reasons to love carrots with me:

  1. They are orange (:

    2. Orange is a really good color. Orange is the color of safety vests and prison jumpsuits because it stands out. This makes carrots (and oranges I suppose) unique.

  2. They have a very distinct carrot shape

    3. The shape of a carrot is incedibly aerodynamic. I've been raised to understand that the more aerodynamic an object is, the better the object is. Aerodyanmics are an important part of the way I live my life.

  3. They vary in size

    4. What is awesomeness without variation? Having a variety of different sizes is awesome! It's better for people who may not want a very large carrot one day, but the next they'll want a huge one!

  4. They are good for your eyesight

    5. Eyesight is a very important part of everyone's daily life. Everyone has to be able to see at least a little. Carrots help with this!

  5. They are grown in gardens!

    6. There is nothing cheaper than growing your own food at home. Boy do I have some new for you! Carrots can be grown right in your backyard! You'll never have to worry about a lack of carrots again!
